Cities of China

China is a country of immense variety in its terrain and geography, but the story of modern China is often told through its cities. According to Public Radio International, for the past two decades China has built twenty new cities each year. These urban centers draw people from China's countryside and from around the world to take advantage of new economic opportunities.
